In English

Tax planning is an activity in which a taxable entity, company or individual seeks to anticipate the tax consequences for them in various situations and seeks to minimize its tax through planning. In planning, the taxpayer chooses the most cost-effective means for him from the various options. Tax planning is an activity approved by the taxpayer that complies with Finnish law. It should not be confused with tax evasion. Our company offers individual, high-quality and effortless tax planning services to individuals, communities and companies.
